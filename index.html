About me

I'm Mel DeJesus. I live in Durham, NC. As a youngster, I had a fondness for an early form of the BASIC computer language.

In a roundabout way, this led me through a brief stint in an Ohio engineering program before I switched to literature and philosophy, both of which I still have a deep affinity for.

Later, I spent time teaching classes in writing and research at various NC colleges, exploring the ways that society and media inform, persuade and motivate us.

This interest in the various kinds of landscapes that engage us led me to work as an outdoor guide at an eco-contemplative center, and I further explored this outdoor motif at a wildcrafting & outdoor school, where I facilitated its sales, operational, and communications strategies.

At present, I've revisited my fascination with writing code, exploring various front-end web development and other forms of software. Coding allows me to integrate many of my past interests while exploring new forms of expression.
